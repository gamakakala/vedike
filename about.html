Some Gamakis living in North America have been perfoming since 1970-80s. In August of 2020 enthusiasts, Gamaki's and organizers have planned to get together and form this association to work further on the promotion and performance of this art of Gamaka in North America. We have compiled some known peformances - a rough history etc. Please send more information so we can update this. This is a volunteer organization open to all.

People/Co-ordination

Convener - ಸಂಯೋಜಕರು
Ragu Kattinakere

Shrishrikanth Toronto and Geetha and Y Dattari are helping co-ordinate. We will open up and welcome more people.

The aim is to build a community of Gamaki's and Vyaakhyanakaras in north america, co-ordinate and conduct Gamaka Vaachana events working both with North America and Indian artists.

  2. In 2012 Shri Ragu Kattinakere performed Harishandra Kaavya in Kannada Sangha Toronto, Canada (Vachana Vyakhyana). Later only Vaachana in 2013 at Chicago, USA.
